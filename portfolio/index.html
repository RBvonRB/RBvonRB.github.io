about me

I am a content creator in the fields of creative coding, animation and interaction design. My passion lies in creating innovative and functional applications for all kinds of challenges. I love to discover and learn new tools and bring together visual elements with audio experiences.

I'm co-founder, project manager and interaction and motion designer at Nachtlicht Creative GmbH in Zürich. I finished my Bachelor in Visual Communication at the Academy of Art and Design Basel in Summer 2020.

Mentoring: Ludwig Zeller

Spark AR is an application provided by Facebook to create Augmented Reality effects and publish them on Facebook and Instagram.

The goal of this semester project was to create AR effects beoynd the usual filters one can find on Social Media and to experiment with this new tool. The effects should be purely typographical/graphical or use the camera input and manipulate it.

I was very interested in the ability to recognize facial movements as well as the possibility to detect and recontextualize colors from the camera feed.
